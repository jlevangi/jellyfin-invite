This page walks you through signing in, watching media, requesting new movies and shows, and getting Jellyfin onto your phone or TV.

Jellyfin

Watch movies and shows

Jellyfin is where you watch the movies and shows already available in Pierce's library.

1
Open Jellyfin.
{{ jellyfin_url }}
2
Sign in with your media account.
Use the email and password from your account setup. If you used Google when creating the account, choose Google again.
3
Browse or search.
Pick a movie or show, then press Play.

TV login works differently

Phones, tablets, and computers can open the normal browser login directly. TVs and streaming devices are harder with a remote, so use QuickConnect.

1
Sign in to Jellyfin on your phone, tablet, or computer first.
2
Open the Jellyfin app on the TV and choose QuickConnect or Quick Connect.
3
Enter the TV code from your already-signed-in phone or computer, then approve the connection.

Troubleshooting

  • If the app asks for a server address, use {{ jellyfin_url }}.
  • Create and test your account on a phone, tablet, or computer before trying a TV.
  • If playback is slow, lower playback quality in the player settings.
Requests

Request movies and shows

Requests is where you ask for media that is not already available in Jellyfin.

1
Open Requests.
{{ requests_url }}
2
Sign in with your media account.
Use the same account as Jellyfin.
3
Search for a movie or show.
Open the result and select Request.

What happens next

  • Pending means waiting for approval or processing.
  • Approved means accepted and waiting to become available.
  • Available means ready to watch in Jellyfin.

Popular releases may appear in about 30 minutes. Niche items can take a day or two.

Mobile shortcut

Add Requests or Jellyfin to your home screen

For quick access, add each website to your phone's home screen like an app.

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open the site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.

Android

  1. Open the site in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu.
  3. Choose Add to Home screen or Install app.
  4. Confirm.
